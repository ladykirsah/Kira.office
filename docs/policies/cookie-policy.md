### 1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit our website. They help the website function correctly, remember your preferences, and understand how the site is used.

### 2. Cookie Categories We Use

| Category | Purpose | Examples | Legal Basis (PDPA) |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Strictly Necessary** | Shopping cart, login, checkout flow, site security | session/cart cookie | Necessary for contract performance (Section 24) — no consent required |
| **Analytics** | Traffic measurement, usage behavior, site improvement (e.g. Google Analytics) | `_ga`, `_gid` | Consent (Section 19) |
| **Marketing/Advertising** | Ad performance tracking, retargeting, customer persona building (e.g. Meta Pixel, TikTok Pixel) | `_fbp`, `_ttp` | Specific consent (Section 19) |
| **Third-party embeds** | LINE Official Account widget, payment processing (PromptPay / SlipOK) | provider-defined | Consent, or necessity to provide the service you requested, as applicable |

### 3. Consent

- On first visit, a consent banner appears; non-essential cookies stay inactive until you choose.
- You can select **"Accept All"**, **"Reject All"** (both equally prominent), or **"Manage Preferences"** to opt in by category.
- Strictly necessary cookies always run and cannot be disabled.
- Your consent choice and timestamp are logged as evidence of consent, as required by law.
- You may change your cookie preferences at any time via the **"Cookie Settings"** link in the site footer.
