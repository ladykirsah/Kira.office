Den Air Service Co., Ltd. ("we") is the data controller for personal data collected through the AirPlus online store, under the Personal Data Protection Act B.E. 2562 (PDPA).

**We do not store** your card numbers or ID-card copies, and **we do not collect sensitive personal data** (race, religion, health data, etc.) under Section 26.

### 3. Purposes and Legal Basis for Processing

| Purpose | Data used | Legal basis |
|---|---|---|
| Fulfilling and delivering your order | Name, phone, address, order history | Necessary to perform a contract (Section 24) |
| Verifying your identity at sign-up/login | Phone number (OTP) | Necessary to perform a contract |
| Confirming you meet the 20+ minimum age | Self-declaration, or date of birth if provided | Legal obligation / necessity for a valid contract |
| Calculating a trust score for Cash-on-Delivery (COD) eligibility | Order history | Our legitimate interest (Section 24) — managing risk from refused/failed COD deliveries |
| Customer-persona analysis for marketing, and sending promotional messages (e.g. birthday offers, via LINE/SMS/email) | Date of birth (if provided), order history | **Consent** — optional, and withdrawable at any time |
| Analytics and advertising cookies | See our Cookie Policy | Consent |

> **Important — the trust score directly affects you:** if your score is low, the system will **automatically reject Cash on Delivery** as a payment option. Other payment methods (bank transfer/PromptPay/card) remain available.

### 4. Disclosure to Third Parties

| Recipient | Data received | Reason |
|---|---|---|
| Delivery carriers (Kerry, Flash Express, Thailand Post) | Name, address, phone | To deliver your order |
| SMS OTP provider | Phone number | Identity verification |
| SlipOK (payment slip verification) | The slip's QR data and amount only (no personal data) | Payment confirmation — **not active yet** |

We do not sell your personal data to third parties.

### 5. Retention

We keep your data for as long as you remain a customer, and delete it on request — except records the law requires us to retain for a set period (e.g., tax invoices/receipts), which we keep as legally required even if you request deletion.

### 6. Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under the PDPA, you have the right to:

- **Access** — request a copy of the data we hold about you
- **Rectification** — request correction of inaccurate data
- **Erasure** — request deletion of your data (subject to the legal exception in §5)
- **Restriction of processing**
- **Data portability**
- **Object** — including objecting to marketing use or to the trust-score calculation
- **Withdraw consent** at any time, without affecting the lawfulness of processing done before withdrawal

**How to exercise these rights:** most of these you can do yourself via your self-service account (view, edit, delete). You can also ask us to check or act on your behalf via the contact channels below, including our LINE OA.

If you're unsatisfied with our response, you have the right to complain to the **Personal Data Protection Committee (PDPC)**.

### 7. Security Measures

We restrict staff access to customer data by role (owner / staff / technician, based on job need), and we do not store your payment-card or ID-card details in our systems.

### 8. Changes to This Notice

We may update this notice from time to time. Material changes will be announced on the website.
