## English Version

### 1. Scope

This policy applies to all products sold through AirPlus. Each product's return/claim/warranty period **varies by category and is stated on that product's detail page**; that single period covers both returns and warranty claims.

### 2. What's Covered and What Isn't

**Covered:**
- Factory defects/damage
- Transit damage (see Terms of Service §7 — we take responsibility directly, regardless of whether it was our packing or the carrier's handling)
- Product not matching its listed description

**Not covered:**
- Customer selecting the wrong vehicle model themselves (treated as "change of mind" if still within the return window)
- Damage from improper installation/use by the customer or a third-party mechanic

### 3. How to Open a Claim

1. Click the cancel/claim button on your order and choose a reason: **Change of mind** or **Damaged/defective product**.
2. **Attach photos of the issue** (and a short video if you suspect transit damage) — required to open the claim.
3. You'll receive our shop's return address, and provide your bank account details for a refund if applicable.
4. Return shipping, both ways: **Change of mind** — you pay / **Damaged/defective** — we pay (based on the reason stated when the claim is opened; even if the final inspection outcome differs, we won't claw back shipping costs already covered).

### 4. Technician Inspection Process

Once the product arrives at our shop, our technician inspects it. We record the inspection process starting from unboxing, as supporting evidence for the decision. There are three possible outcomes:

| Outcome | Meaning |
|---|---|
| **Fully approved (100%)** | Confirmed as a covered defect/damage. You get full resolution (see §5 — choose refund or replacement). |
| **Partially approved** | You receive a partial refund; we'll state the percentage and the reason, case by case. |
| **Rejected (0%)** | Doesn't qualify for a claim, with a stated reason. We ship the product back to you (it remains your property) — no refund is issued. |

### 5. Resolution Choice for Fully Approved Claims (Warranty)

You may choose between:
- **A full refund**, or
- **A replacement product** — must be the identical model/specification; no substituting a different product or spec.

### 6. Refund Timing

Once a decision is made, you'll receive any refund to your registered bank account within 2–3 business days.
